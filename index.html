A Passion for the Waterways

Founded by lifelong paddlers, Mangrove Lakemount Kayak was born out of a deep love for the untouched beauty of Nedungolam, Paravoor's mangrove ecosystems and freshwater lakes. We believe everyone deserves to experience the serenity of gliding through still water, surrounded by towering cypress and ancient mangrove roots.

Our certified guides bring expertise, enthusiasm, and a genuine care for your safety. Whether you're a first-time paddler or an experienced kayaker, we tailor every experience to match your pace and comfort level.

  • Certified & experienced local guides
  • Top-quality kayaks & safety gear provided
  • Small group sizes for a personal experience
  • Leave-no-trace environmental philosophy
